Sweet Potato Fries

Fried sweet potato features in a variety of dishes and cuisines including the
popular sweet potato fries, a variation of French fries using sweet potato
instead of potato

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes, cut into large French fries
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • Â½ teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place sweet potatoes and water into a microwave-safe dish. Cook in the
    microwave on full power for 5 minutes; drain. Add olive oil, Italian
    seasoning, lemon pepper, salt, and pepper; toss to coat.
  3. Arrange fries on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven, turning once, until fries are crispy on the
    outside, about 30 minutes.
